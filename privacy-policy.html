Privacy Policy

Scientist in the Loop

Introduction

Last Updated: April 23, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Scientist in the Loop ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares your personal information when you visit our website, join our waitlist, or interact with our services.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Email address when you join our waitlist.
  • Quiz Data: Your answers to our quiz questions, including your score and response patterns.
  • Consent Information: Records of your consent choices, including timestamps.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with our website, including IP address, browser type, pages visited, and time spent on the site.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • To add you to our waitlist and notify you when our services launch
  • To analyze quiz responses and improve our educational content
  • To send marketing communications (only with your explicit consent)
  • To maintain and improve our website
  • To comply with legal obligations

Legal Basis for Processing (EU Users)

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), we process your personal data based on the following legal grounds:

  • Consent: We collect and process your data based on the specific consents you provide.
  • Legitimate Interest: We may process certain data to improve our services and protect our legal rights.
  • Legal Obligation: We may process data to comply with applicable laws.

Data Sharing and Third Parties

We may share your information with:

  • Service providers who help us operate our website and services
  • Third-party marketing partners (only with your explicit consent)
  • Legal authorities when required by law

Data Storage and Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized or unlawful processing, accidental loss, destruction, or damage.

Your Data Protection Rights

Under the GDPR and other applicable data protection laws, you have the following rights:

  • Access: You can request copies of your personal data.
  • Rectification: You can ask us to correct inaccurate information or complete incomplete information.
  • Erasure: You can ask us to delete your personal data in certain circumstances.
  • Restriction: You can ask us to restrict the processing of your data in certain circumstances.
  • Data Portability: You can ask us to transfer your data to another organization or directly to you.
  • Objection: You can object to how we process your data in certain circumstances.
  • Withdraw Consent: You can withdraw your consent at any time for processing based on consent.

Cookies and Similar Technologies

Our website uses cookies and similar technologies to enhance your browsing experience and analyze website traffic. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
